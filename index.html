MARS

Freedom zone

Mars stands as humanity’s boldest experiment in colonization, a world carved from dust and ambition. Once a desolate wasteland, it is now the beating heart of the Freedom Zone — the shared sphere of Earth and Mars where civilians, traders, and dreamers can move freely between worlds. The planet is divided into vast territories claimed by the great superpowers, yet beneath the banners of Earth’s nations simmers a restless desire for independence. Towering domed cities, sprawling mining colonies, and militarized frontiers make Mars both a land of opportunity and a crucible of conflict. For settlers, it offers escape from Earth’s decaying economies. For corporations, it is a resource to be controlled. For rebels, it is the first step toward breaking free of Earth’s grasp.
